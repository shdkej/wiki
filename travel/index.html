Hungary

Well I myself liked Budapest the most. It has the most choices. However if you like mountains and hills you should check out Kékes once. It's not the tallest mountain but it's looks beautiful. Also Balaton lake is popular but it's also expensive for even foreigners. Hévíz is a thermal lake. I think it's a better place to go to. Be be sure that you can swim because it's 90 meters deep. Poland

바르샤바는 폴란드의 수도이니까 아마 가장 유명 한거예요. 제가 사는 도시 이름은 크라쿠프예요, 여기도 인기있는 관광이에요. 크라쿠프는 아름다운 도시예요. 자연을 좋아한다면 자코파네를 방문하기에 완벽한 장소이다. 그리고 역사를 좋아한다면 Auschwitz를 추천해요.
